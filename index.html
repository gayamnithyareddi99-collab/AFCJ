About AFCJ Ltd

AFCJ Ltd is a UK-based B2B SaaS platform delivering privacy-preserving recruitment analytics for agencies, RPOs, and enterprise talent teams. The company solves a structural industry problem: organisations cannot retain long-term hiring intelligence without accumulating sensitive candidate data that increases compliance and security risk.

Modern Applicant Tracking Systems routinely delete candidate journey records within 12‚Äì18 months, removing multi-year visibility into funnel performance, recruiter effectiveness, and hiring trends. To compensate, many organisations create shadow datasets in BI tools and spreadsheets, multiplying personal data exposure across systems.

AFCJ replaces this fragile model with a privacy-engineered analytics infrastructure that preserves behavioural signal without retaining identifiable records. Instead of storing raw candidate journeys, the platform converts recruitment events into irreversible analytical representations that remain usable for long-term trend analysis, benchmarking, and forecasting.

The platform integrates directly with ATS and CRM systems, enabling multi-source data fusion under GDPR-aligned governance controls. Cohort-level safeguards, behavioural uniqueness thresholds, and contract-aware enforcement ensure that analytics remain compliant, auditable, and secure.

The AFCJ Intelligence Process

AFCJ begins with secure, contract-aware integration across Applicant Tracking Systems and recruitment CRM environments. Rather than extracting raw candidate records for long-term storage, the platform isolates behavioural recruitment signals within controlled governance boundaries.

These recruitment lifecycle events are transformed into anonymised analytical representations engineered to preserve decision-making intelligence while eliminating identity retention. This ensures that organisations retain performance insight without accumulating regulatory exposure.

Cohort-level safeguards, uniqueness thresholds, and GDPR-aligned enforcement mechanisms operate continuously, ensuring audit resilience and compliance stability across long-term workforce planning horizons.

The result is executive-ready recruitment intelligence that supports forecasting, optimisation, and AI readiness without compromising privacy integrity.
